Guidelines & Steps to Participate

  1. Work must be original, unpublished. Plagiarism or re-posted content is strictly not allowed.
  2. Avoid AI-generated content — let your own insights shine!
  3. Language: English only this edition.
  4. One submission per author/team; you can revise and resubmit till deadline.
  5. Max 3 pages per article. Cite references as needed.
  6. Submissions are reviewed by Wayanad House core team; decisions are final.
  7. Shortlisted articles will be featured in our newsletter (with author credits & certificates for all entries).

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I submit an article as a team?

Yes! Collaborative articles are encouraged, especially if you participated as a team in previous ML contests.

Can I update or revise my article after initial submission?

Absolutely! You can revise and resubmit your article any time before the deadline.

Are code snippets or diagrams allowed?

Yes, as long as they are relevant and clearly presented, and you cite any sources if not original.

Do I get a certificate for participation?

Yes! Every submitted article (meeting guidelines) earns a digital certificate from Wayanad House.

How will I be notified if my article is selected?

You’ll get an email at your registered address once selections are announced.
